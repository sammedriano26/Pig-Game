Welcome to the Pig Game!

Hi! I'm Gemini Coder! I wish to thank you for checking my code and trying out this awesome game. Let me show how this game works.

  1. 1. This game has to be played by two players.
  2. 2. First step is to set your "Winning Score" on the input field provided.
  3. 3. The first player can click on the "Roll" button as many times as he like, and his scores will be added automatically under "Current Score".
  4. 4.The scores will continue to add up unless you rolled a "1". In which case, the score will reset to "zero" and the game continues with Player 2 now being the active player.
  5. 5. If you wish to hold your current score, simply press the "Hold" button, and your score will automatically be added to your "Global Score" located right above the "Current Score". Doing so will change the active player status to Player 2 and vice versa.
  6. 6. Please note though that when you roll a 6 on both dice, your Global Score will reset to zero and player 2 will become active.
  7. 7. Please note though that when you roll a 6 on both dice, your Global Score will reset to zero and player 2 will become active, vice versa.
  8. 8. The same goes when your dice total is 6 for two consecutive rolls.
  9. 9. Whoever gets the set points first on his/her Global Score wins the game.
